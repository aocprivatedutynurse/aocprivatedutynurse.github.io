About us

The healthcare field is changing rapidly, each year bringing important advances in research, education, technology, treatments and practice that challenge nurses to provide the best care possible for all patients. At the AOC Private Duty Nurse, we ensure that our students keep pace with the changes and challenges by fostering an atmosphere of learning that has earned us a national and international reputation for excellence in teaching, research and clinical practice.

The AOC Private Duty Nurse – Nursing Reimagined. Nursing Redefined.

Vision

The AOC Private Duty Nurse is a significant force in developing nurse leaders to inspire individuals across the lifespan to achieve health, wellness and quality of life.

Mission

The AOC Private Duty Nurse prepares nurses and scholars to lead and transform nursing care in a rapidly changing, diverse and complex healthcare environment through academic excellence, innovative research, superior clinical practice, strong community partnerships, and global initiatives.

Goals

Caring for patients with acute and chronic illnesses; facilitating discharge planning; providing palliative care; and offering patient education; illness prevention services, and health maintenance care. Providing comprehensive care that considers the patient's social, emotional, cultural, and physical needs.
